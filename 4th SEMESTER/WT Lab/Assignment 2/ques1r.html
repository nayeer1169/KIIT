We Take Care Of Your Health

We are committed to providing access to affordable and innovative medicines,
driven by our purpose of ‘Good Health Can’t Wait’.
Our products and services are spread across our core
businesses of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (API)
, generics, branded generics, biosimilars and over-the-counter
pharmaceutical products around the world.
We work towards meeting unmet
patients needs in the areas of gastro-enterology,
cardiovascular, diabetology, oncology, pain management and dermatology.
We are investing in businesses of the future including drug discovery,
clinically-differentiated assets and digital healthcare.
